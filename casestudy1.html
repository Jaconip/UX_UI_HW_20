As part of our course requirement, we were tasked as a group to redesign the mobile and desktop version of a nonprofit organization’s website in 3 weeks.



We were 3 members in our group and we have chosen WetlandCare Australia as its website has not been updated since 2017. All three members had equal share for roles in UX research, UI design and prototyping.






PROBLEM STATEMENT

The current design features cumbersome graphics and buttons, and crucial information is not readily accessible, resulting in an unfavorable portrayal of the organisation.



VALUE PROPOSITION

The redesigned site goes beyond a donation platform, fosters community engagement and encourages active participation from users of all ages.




  • Monthly and One-Off are separated into two different buttons and Donation Type heading is added to make it more clear that the user action is needed.
  • Added option to input the donation amount.
  • Users are able to navigate through the years and spend as much time as they want analysing the data.
  • X or Close button - Increased length by 10px and thickness from 3px to 5px.
  • Changed text from ‘learn more’ to ‘See Case Studies’ and changed colour to black to increase contrast.
  • Changed text from ‘Donation type’ to ‘Choose donation type’.
  • Increased font size of mission statement from 32px to 40px and centred text.
  • Aligned case study headings
  • Changed images to increase text contrast.
  • Added arrows to create call-to-action.
  • Inserted button to remain consistent with web view.
